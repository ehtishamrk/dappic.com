Frequently Asked Questions

Why wouldn't I just hire a full-time designer?

A full-time senior designer costs $100k+ annually. Our subscription provides an entire team of specialists for a fraction of the cost with complete flexibility.

Why wouldn't I just hire another agency or freelancer?

Traditional agencies require lengthy contracts with hidden fees. Freelancers often lack reliability. We provide consistent quality with transparent pricing and the flexibility to cancel anytime.

What types of designs can you create?

We handle everything from brand identity, web design, UI/UX, motion graphics, to marketing materials. Each tier includes different service levels detailed in the package features.

What is unlimited design and unlimited revisions?

Submit as many design requests as needed. We work on them sequentially based on your priority. Revisions continue until you're completely satisfied with the result.

How do I request designs?

Simply submit requests through our platform, Slack, email, or your preferred communication channel. Your dedicated manager handles the rest.

How fast will I receive my designs?

Most requests are completed within 48-72 hours. Complex projects may take longer, but we'll always provide clear timelines upfront.

Who are the designers?

Our team consists of senior designers with 10+ years experience from top agencies and tech companies, carefully vetted for quality and reliability.

How does the pause feature work?

Pause your subscription anytime. Billing stops immediately and resumes only when you're ready to continue. Perfect for seasonal businesses.

What if I don't like the design?

We offer unlimited revisions until you're satisfied. If we truly can't meet your needs, you can cancel anytime without penalty.

Are there any refunds?

Due to the nature of our service, we don't offer refunds. However, you can cancel anytime and we guarantee satisfaction through unlimited revisions.
