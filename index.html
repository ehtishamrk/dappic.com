Referral Program Terms

Effective Date: November 14, 2025

1. Program Overview

Our Referral Program rewards you for introducing Dappic to new clients. Earn 10% monthly recurring commission for every successful referral.

2. Eligibility

Anyone can participate in our referral program. Existing customers and partners are welcome to refer new clients.

3. Commission Structure

You earn 10% of the referred client's monthly subscription fee for as long as they remain an active customer. If you're already a Dappic customer, commission will be credited toward your account balance rather than paid in cash.

4. Payment Terms

Cash commissions are paid monthly via your preferred method (PayPal, bank transfer, or wire). Account credits for existing customers are applied automatically to reduce your next invoice.

5. Qualified Referrals

A referral is qualified when: (1) The referred party signs up for a paid subscription, (2) They complete their first month of service, (3) They were not already in contact with Dappic prior to your referral.

6. Referral Tracking

You'll receive a unique referral link and code. All referrals must use your link or code to ensure proper tracking and commission credit.

7. Restrictions

Self-referrals are prohibited. Fraudulent activity results in immediate program termination and forfeiture of all commissions.

8. Program Changes

Dappic reserves the right to modify or terminate the referral program at any time with 30 days notice. Existing commissions will be honored.

9. Contact

Questions about the referral program? Email us at referrals@dappic.com
